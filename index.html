How It Works
Three steps to own your narrative

From the moment a claim surfaces to the moment your truth becomes the record, Verilay keeps you in control.

01

A Claim Surfaces

News articles, social media posts, or rumours mention you or your brand online. Verilay's Radar detects them instantly across every major platform.

02

You Respond

Accept, deny, or modify the claim with your own verified statement on Verilay. Add context, correct facts, or set the record straight — in your words.

03

Truth Spreads

Your verified response becomes the public record. The community vouches for it, building an immutable trust score that follows the claim everywhere.

Features
Built for the truth economy

Every tool you need to monitor, respond to, and permanently resolve claims about you or your brand.

Radar

Real-time monitoring across Google News, Reddit, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, and Instagram. Know the moment someone mentions your name.

Shield

One-click response cards, denial statements, takedown requests, and evidence exports. Respond professionally in seconds, not hours.

Trust Score

A community-driven vouch and counter system that builds your credibility over time. The more you engage, the stronger your truth profile becomes.

Truth Profile

Your permanent, verifiable record of every claim addressed. A single page that tells the world exactly where you stand — forever.
